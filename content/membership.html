Membership

LOFFA Membership

Membership of the organisation is open to Families with or affected by ASD in Laois Offaly area. Membership will be by subscription basis for a calendar year. Membership Rules and Subscriptions amounts will be reviewed and approved at the Annual General Meeting. (AGM)

  • Failure to renew membership by March 31st each year will result in loss of voting rights at the next Annual General meeting
Membership Entitlement
  • Re-imbursement of approved therapies for Members as deemed by the Committee

Note: All expenses must be documented and approved by the committee in advance.
